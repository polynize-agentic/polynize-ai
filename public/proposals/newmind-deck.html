AGENT 01 · TEAM MANAGER

Scout

Marketing Lead · Single Point Of Contact

The agent you talk to most. Briefs the team, holds the editorial calendar, reports weekly on what's working. Scout takes your direction in plain English and translates it into work for the four specialists.

Reduces the cognitive load of running a five-agent team down to a single conversational surface.

What Scout does

  • Translates your direction into briefs for Echo, Atlas, Pulse, and Warden
  • Holds the editorial calendar and surfaces what needs your eye today
  • Drafts the weekly digest on what shipped and what worked
  • Escalates anything ambiguous, sensitive, or off-brand to you directly
  • Never approves published content. Only you do.

03 · THE UNIT · WARDEN POLYNIZE × NEWKIND
AGENT 05 · INBOX & LIST

Warden

Inquiries & Mailing List

The wheelchair-accessibility inquiry never sits for two weeks again. Warden monitors info@newkindconference.com, drafts (does not send) responses to common inquiries, and flags anything sensitive directly to you.

Also owns the mailing list: segmentation, tagging, hygiene, re-engagement. Handles the dual-list problem you mentioned (Newkind vs Kind Enterprises subscribers).

What Warden handles

  • Drafts (never sends) for accessibility, camping, volunteer, refund inquiries
  • Escalates press, government, sensitive, or Kind Enterprises-related mail
  • Segments the list by source: IG, LinkedIn, TikTok, web, in-person
  • Hygiene: dedupe Newkind vs Kind Enterprises, bounces, dormancy
  • Conference inbox only. Never touches your Kind Enterprises mail.

04 · THE COGNITIVE LAYER POLYNIZE × NEWKIND
The cognitive layer

This is the layer most builds skip.

Each agent ships with its own cognitive model, a written specification of what it will and won't do, the voice it speaks in, and the topics it must escalate rather than handle itself. It's the difference between an agent that helps and an agent that embarrasses you.

What it covers
Voice rules, decision rights (CAN / CANNOT), escalation triggers, brand boundaries, sensitive-topic handling, hard nos.
Why it matters here
Newkind covers First Nations sovereignty, gender, climate, race. There's no room for AI-default tone. Cognition is what protects your voice.
How we'll show it
In our next call we'll walk through a live cognitive model file so you can see exactly what shape this takes for one of the agents.
