Agent 01
Scout
Content & Social
The content arm. Human sets strategy. Scout executes continuously.
  • Manages all 4 channels simultaneously
  • Runs main account + sub-Persona accounts
  • A/B tests narrative angles weekly
  • Signals when a pillar is ready to scale
Agent 02
Lure
Lead Gen & Capture
The capture arm. Human lead sees qualified leads. Lure handles every step that gets them there.
  • ManyChat comment-trigger flows
  • Household Readiness Quiz delivery
  • Scores and buckets leads by tier
  • Routes to matching funnel automatically
Agent 03
Ember
Website Agent
The inbound arm. No unqualified inquiry reaches the team. Ember handles it first.
  • Live chat on everstock.com.au, 24/7
  • Trained on full brand and product range
  • Pre-qualifies every visitor before handoff
  • Passes structured lead data to Lure
Agent 04
Pulse
Analytics & Signal
The intelligence arm. Human makes the call. Pulse makes sure it's an informed one.
  • Monitors all channels every week
  • Generates SEO content continuously
  • Friday report: data, interpretation, rec
  • Drives the day-30 go/no-go call
The cognitive unit: One human lead at Optio directs a layer of agents that handle the high-volume work underneath. The human makes the calls. The agents execute, report back, and adapt. One coordinated unit with a human at the top.
